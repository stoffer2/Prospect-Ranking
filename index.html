Our Methodology

Borda Count Aggregation

We analyze rankings from MLB Pipeline, Baseball America, FanGraphs, Bleacher Report, ESPN, and Just Baseball using the Borda Count method. Each prospect receives points based on their ranking position (#1 = 100 points, #2 = 99 points, etc.). We sum points across all sources to create our consensus rankings.

Why This Works Better

Unlike simple averaging, Borda Count handles missing data elegantly and naturally weights top rankings more heavily. A prospect ranked #1 by two sources and #25 by another deserves to rank higher than one with consistent mid-range rankings - and our algorithm captures that.
